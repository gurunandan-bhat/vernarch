The Vernacular Architecture of India

Traditional Residential Styles and Spaces

— Tejinder S. Randhawa

An encyclopedic compendium, based on decades of research, travel, documenting and photographing the indigenous architecture of the people of India.

Illustrates and documents the vast diversity of social and environmental adaptations and preferences in dwellings, built from locally available material with locally available skills.

550 pages, over 1600 high-resolution color photographs, plates and spreads covering six regions and four research essays (click to read excerpts) on different aspects of traditional architecture of India
