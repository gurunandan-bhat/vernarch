The Vernacular Architecture of India

Tejinder S. Randhawa

An encyclopedic compendium, based on over a decade of research and travel, documenting and photographing the indigenous architecture of the people of India.

The book captures the vast diversity of social and environmental needs and preferences, in dwellings built from locally available material with locally available skills.
